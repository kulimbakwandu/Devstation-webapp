About DevStation
Initiative

Founded in 2025, DevStation is a Tanzanian non-profit initiative dedicated to empowering local developers through skills development, community building, and career support. We believe in the transformative power of technology education to create economic opportunities.

Our programs aim to train over 500 developers across Tanzania per year, with many securing positions at leading tech companies or launching their own startups.
